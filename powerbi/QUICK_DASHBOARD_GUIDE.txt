
🎯 QUICK START GUIDE - POWER BI DASHBOARD
=========================================

YOUR DATA IS READY! Here's how to create the 5-page dashboard:

STEP 1: IMPORT DATA (2 minutes)
--------------------------------
1. Open Power BI Desktop
2. Click 'Get Data' → 'Text/CSV' 
3. Navigate to: ../powerbi/data/
4. Load ALL 5 CSV files

STEP 2: CREATE 5 PAGES (15 minutes)
-----------------------------------

📊 PAGE 1: Executive Overview
• 4 Card Visuals: Total Customers, High Risk Count, Expected Loss, Model AUC
• Pie Chart: Risk segment distribution
• Gauge: Average churn probability

🔍 PAGE 2: Risk Analysis
• Bar Chart: Customers by risk segment  
• Scatter Plot: Probability vs Expected Loss
• Table: High-risk customer details

🎯 PAGE 3: Driver Analysis
• Horizontal Bar: Top 10 features by SHAP importance
• Treemap: Driver categories
• Waterfall: Feature impact

👤 PAGE 4: Individual Analysis  
• Slicers: Risk segment, region, account type
• Customer details table
• Individual risk cards

💰 PAGE 5: Business Impact
• Cards: Total loss, high-risk loss, savings potential
• Stacked Bar: Loss by segment
• Matrix: Region × Segment × Loss

STEP 3: FORMAT & SAVE (3 minutes)
---------------------------------
• Use colors: Red=High Risk, Yellow=Medium, Green=Low
• Add descriptive titles
• Save as: bank_churn_dashboard.pbix

⏱️ TOTAL TIME: 20 minutes
🎉 YOUR DASHBOARD WILL BE READY!
